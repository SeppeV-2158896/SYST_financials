Scenario 3: Review Historical Data and Set Up a Meeting for Dave
-----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the application and navigate to the login page.
2. Enter the following credentials:
   - Email: dave@example.com
   - Password: dave123
3. Click the "Login" button.
4. After logging in, navigate to the "Financial Overview" page.
5. Review the historical financial data, including:
   - Income for each academic year.
   - Study expenses (tuition, books, housing) for each academic year.
   - Financial aid received (scholarships, grants) for each academic year.
6. Analyze how the study costs and study income have changed over the years.
7. Navigate back to the account info and click on "Submit".
8. Apply for an UHasselt aid through UHasselt social service via email.
